Motorcycle trip down the Baja California peninsula. Sightseeing, whale watching, beach wandering, and fish taco eating. Predominantly riding paved roads on street tires.

*Suitable for larger adventure bikes, Expect up to 40 miles of well groomed dirt roads. Tires should be good for 3600 miles.*
